The Artist

Behind the Canvas

I am an artist and developer exploring the intersection of precise logic and chaotic beauty. When I'm not writing tools for the Unity engine at Liocorn, I pour my creative energy into physical mediums. My work contrasts the strict architectures of code with the unpredictable nature of fluid acrylics, resin, and gold leaf.
